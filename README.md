## How Fast Is It Really?

Ownr markets the "incorporate in 15 minutes" angle pretty hard. Is it true?

Sort of. The actual time *you* spend filling out forms and answering questions is probably 15-30 minutes. But then Ownr has to process everything, submit it to the government, and wait for approval.

Actual turnaround times:
- **Numbered company** (no custom name): Usually 1 business day
- **Named company in Ontario**: 1-5 business days  
- **Named company in Alberta**: 1 business day (after ID verification)
- **Named company in BC**: 7 business days (or 1 day with expedited fee)
- **Federal incorporation**: 1-5 business days

So while your part is quick, you're not getting incorporated in 15 minutes flat unless you go with a numbered company. Still faster than the traditional lawyer route, which can take weeks.
